About Indie Fest

The beautiful city of Portland, Oregon will be the host city for Indie Fest!

Hosting 60,000 attendees of all ages from 45 states and 26 countries, Indie Music Festival offers a wide array of activities beyond the music. With 50 individual vendors, as well as specialty record and craft fairs, the festival works to support local businesses while promoting the Portland arts and food communities as a whole.

About Indie Fest

Celebrating its 14th year in 2019, the internationally recognized Indie Music Festival presents 40+ acts over the course of three days each summer in Portland's Laurelhurst Park.

The festival highlights the best in new and emerging music. Since 2006, this independently run festival has consistently proven to be one of the most welcoming, comfortable, reasonably priced, and rewarding weekends of music around.
